Manuscript and Publication Notes

Conference acceptance, presentation, and registration do not automatically imply publication. Papers recommended for proceedings or special issue consideration will undergo additional editorial and peer-review checks according to publisher or journal requirements.

Manuscript length: 6–8 pages for proceedings paper, or as per publisher template.

Similarity limit: preferably below 15–20%, excluding references, depending on publisher policy.

Template: Springer proceedings template, if LNME proposal is approved.

Review result: accepted for oral/poster presentation; accepted with revision; rejected.
